# Company & Competitive News Memo — Last 12 Months

## COMPANY SNAPSHOT
- **Company:** First Due
- **Industry:** Fire & EMS / public safety software (RMS and data platforms)
- **HQ / Geography:** Miami, Florida, USA
- **Product / Business Model:** AI-powered fire and EMS RMS software suite sold to public safety agencies.

## COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
- **Primary Competitors:**
  - ESO Solutions — EMS data, ePCR, and analytics platform for agencies and state repositories.
  - ImageTrend — EMS/fire reporting, analytics, and compliance software.
  - CentralSquare — public safety CAD/RMS and cloud public safety suite used by fire and municipal agencies.

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (3–4 bullets max)
- First Due accelerated product expansion across prevention, training, analytics, and adjacent public safety markets in 2025, signaling a broader platform play.
- A $355M strategic investment provides substantial capital for scaling product and market reach.
- Compliance milestones (NERIS and NEMSIS 3.5) reinforce data standards readiness, a critical procurement and adoption driver.

## KEY COMPANY DEVELOPMENTS (5–7 bullets max)

- **2025-02-26 — Launches Fire Prevention ITM solution.**
  Why it matters: **Expands into prevention/compliance workflows, adding a new revenue stream beyond response operations.**
  (Source: [A1](https://www.firstdue.com/news/itm-solution))

- **2025-03-18 — Launches scheduling & workforce management platform for law enforcement.**
  Why it matters: **Signals expansion beyond fire/EMS into broader public safety markets.**
  (Source: [A2](https://www.firstdue.com/news/first-due-launches-law-enforcement))

- **2025-04-08 — Launches Learning Management System for Fire & EMS training.**
  Why it matters: **Adds training/compliance to the suite, increasing stickiness and cross-sell potential.**
  (Source: [A3](https://www.firstdue.com/news/first-due-launches-lms))

- **2025-07-21 — Launches Advanced Data Insights analytics.**
  Why it matters: **Strengthens data-driven decision support, a key differentiator in agency purchasing.**
  (Source: [A4](https://www.firstdue.com/news/launches-data-insights))

- **2025-08-05 — Secures $355M strategic investment.**
  Why it matters: **Material capital infusion to accelerate product development, go-to-market, and scale.**
  (Source: [A5](https://www.firstdue.com/news/investment))

- **2025-11-06 — Leads NERIS transition, powering nearly half of submissions.**
  Why it matters: **Demonstrates adoption at scale and positions First Due as a standards leader.**
  (Source: [A6](https://www.firstdue.com/news/first-due-leads-neris-transition))

- **2025-12-04 — Achieves NEMSIS 3.5 Receive & Process compliance.**
  Why it matters: **Improves eligibility in state EMS procurements and reduces compliance friction.**
  (Source: [A7](https://www.firstdue.com/news/nemsis-3-5-compliance))
