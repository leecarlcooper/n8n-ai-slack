Company & Competitive News Memo — Last 12 Months

COMPANY SNAPSHOT

  • Company: First Due
  • Industry: Fire & EMS / public safety software (RMS and data platforms)
  • HQ / Geography: Miami, Florida, USA
  • Product / Business Model: AI-powered fire and EMS RMS software suite sold to public safety agencies.

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

  • Primary Competitors:

- ESO Solutions — EMS data, ePCR, and analytics platform for agencies and state repositories. - ImageTrend — EMS/fire reporting, analytics, and compliance software. - CentralSquare — public safety CAD/RMS and cloud public safety suite used by fire and municipal agencies.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (3–4 bullets max)

  • First Due accelerated product expansion across prevention, training, analytics, and adjacent public safety markets in 2025, signaling a broader platform play.
  • A $355M strategic investment provides substantial capital for scaling product and market reach.
  • Compliance milestones (NERIS and NEMSIS 3.5) reinforce data standards readiness, a critical procurement and adoption driver.

  • 2025-08-05 — Secures $355M strategic investment.

  • 2025-11-06 — Leads NERIS transition, powering nearly half of submissions.
